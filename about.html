About Shale Holdings (Pty Ltd)

Shale Holdings (Pty Ltd) is a leading provider of high-quality construction equipment, catering to the needs of construction companies, contractors, and builders. We specialize in offering a wide range of equipment, including excavators, bulldozers, loaders, cranes, and more.

With years of experience in the industry, we are committed to delivering reliable equipment solutions that meet the demands of modern construction projects. Our team consists of experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing exceptional service and support to our clients.

Our History

  • 1990: Founded with a small inventory of bulldozers and excavators.
  • 2000: Expanded to include crane rentals and maintenance services.
  • 2010: Launched innovative equipment management software.
  • 2020: Grew to serve over 10,000 clients across the country.
